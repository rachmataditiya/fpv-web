# Code of Conduct

This project adapts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) in a concise form.

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of identity, background, or skill level.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be kind and patient.
- Welcome newcomers and diverse perspectives.
- Give credit where it's due.
- Focus on what's best for the project.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any form.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Violence, threats, or sexualised content.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it via [GitHub issues](https://github.com/rachmataditiya/fpv-web/issues) or contact the maintainer directly.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential. Violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.
